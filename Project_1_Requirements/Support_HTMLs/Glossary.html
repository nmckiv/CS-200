ChocAn Project 1 - Requirements

Glossary

Term Definition
Chocoholics Anonymous (ChocAn) Organization helping to free people from chocolate addiction
ChocAn Operator A ChocAn employee that interacts with the provider and member directories
ChocAn Data Center A data center where all of ChocAn's data is held. This center also communicates the data out to others, generally in the form of an email attachment
Acme Accounting Services Third party organization in charge of financial procedures regarding memberships such as recording payments of fees, suspending members with unpaid fees, and reinstating members who have paid what is owed. They work with ChocAn data center to update membership records.
Member An individual who has signed up with ChocAn to use it's services at providers
Member Card A card used to verify if the holder is a member of ChocAn
Member Number A 9-digit code that is used to identify a member and their membership status
Provider A health care professional that is partnered with ChocAn to provide services to members of ChocAn
Provider Directory A database containing all codes and data related to the services each provider offers
Provider's Terminal A computer terminal that is used to validate the member, keep record of the date of consultation, and the service provided
Provider Form A form the provider fills out with info about a service provided for the purpose of later verification
Electronic Funds Transfer (EFT) A transfer of funds from one bank account to another by computer-based systems
EFT Record A file containing the provider name, number and amount to be paid to that provider
Service Code A unique 6-digit code that is used to identify each service that a provider provides to a member
Summary Report This report is given to the accounts payable manager and contains a record of every provider that is to be paid that week, the services and fees billed, and total fees and consultations made that week
Interactive Mode The mode that the ChocAn software is run in during the day so that the member and provider data can be recorded
Member Report Report that is sent as an email attachment to the member, consisting of a list of all services provided that week
Provider Report Report that is sent as an email attachment to providers containing the record of all services provided that week
